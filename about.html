About the Author

Visual Basic and SAP

I started my IT career developing in Visual Basic and MS Access creating inventory management systems for shopkeepers.

Shortly after I had the opportunity to move into larger systems with SAP R/3, developing custom programs using the ABAP language and importing legacy data into SAP databases. Clients were multinational companies in a variety of industries, including FMCG, Engineering, Electronics, Automotive and Oil and reports often needed to be multilingual.

Microsoft Dynamic AX

After several years as a senior consultant in SAP I had the opportunity to move into Microsoft's ERP solution, Axapta (later called MS Dynamics AX). This allowed me to leverage my experience in ERP with my prior knowledge in Visual Basic.

Web Development

I took the opportunity of some down time in 2013 to learn full stack web development, with HTML, CSS, JS and jQuery on the front end and PHP at the back end. The associated SQL database technology was virtually the same as with SAP so was quite easy to pick up.

I subsequently expanded my web development experience with a SANS security course and in order to gain experience with a web framework, took coursework in Ruby on Rails development. I later extended this with coursework in a front end framework, ReactJS
